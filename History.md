# Our History

[![The very first strength training session](/assets/images/theVeryFirstST.jpeg)](/assets/images/theVeryFirstST.jpeg)

*The very first strength training session, November 2017.*
{: .figure-caption}

In November 2017, a few members of the PULA Running Group — one of the local
groups of the Pune running community, who trained near Pu La Deshpande Garden
(hence the name) — came up with the idea of starting strength sessions in
Nanded City. Along with a few runners from the area, they joined the first
strength session in front of the Nanded City marketing office.

The first session was led by Padmaraj Doshi Guruji.
