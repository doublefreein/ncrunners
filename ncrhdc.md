# NCRunners 100-Day Challenge

[![100-Day Challenge badge](/assets/images/ncrhdc/ncrhdc_badge.png)](/assets/images/ncrhdc/ncrhdc_badge.png)

- ✅ Complete 100 days of activity in **110 days**.
- ✅ Choose any one activity per day:
  - 20 mins running
  - 15 mins swimming
  - 30 mins cycling
  - 30 mins trekking
  - 30 mins yoga / strength training / gym / walking
- ✅ Daily attendance is made public. Reply to your previous message with the
  new day count and an attendance picture.
  - `95/102 Swimming` means it's your 102nd day since the start and your 95th activity.
  - Aim to complete 100 activities in 110 days — `100/110` is the boundary line.
- ✅ Finish all 100 days and earn a badge from NCRunners! Finishers are listed
  on the official NCRunners website.

Let's stay consistent and make this happen! **You can begin anytime.**

Join the challenge group on [WhatsApp](https://chat.whatsapp.com/FNmS7vddZ0F6NR5CX36zE8).
